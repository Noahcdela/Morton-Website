Meet the Band

Rebecca

Rebecca Rae (Bex) is a fashion bug who drinks (too much) coffee. She's always on the lookout for a beautiful place to capture through photography and loves to climb abandoned buildings and trespass. Bex was born to adventure-She once planned a camping road trip along the West Coast in less than two hours. She is always setting goals to achieve them. She sees what she wants, and makes it happen. Rebecca is a go-getter and a beautiful combination of confidence and innocence.

Becca plays the banjo and violin.

Mookie

Marissa Kay (Mookie) is the feeler. She has this sort of ambitious anticipation, like she’d wait an eternity for everyone to feel emotions the way she does but she’s not afraid to make you feel it all at once either-you’ll feel it as much as you see it whenever she’s performing on stage. Mookie is the type of person who can make strangers cry just by freestyle singing in somebody’s backyard. she's incapable of hate and impossible to not love, spend a few moments with her and you’ll wonder how you got through the rest of your life without knowing her.

Mookie plays the guitar and mandolin.

Hannah Mae

Hannah Mae, also known as the groovy hipster chick—is the rock of the band. We don't like to say that we have a main leader, but if we did, it would be her. She is bold and has this compelling voice that makes you feel things you may have never felt before. When she's not collecting rocks or working at one of her 10 jobs, she is writing music—bloody good music if I may add. As our main songwriter, she produces music that adds spice and sugar to our tunes. Oh and did I mention she's a world traveler? Ask this lady about her story, you're gonna wanna hear it.

Hannah plays the ukulele and the guitar.
